# Maximo Maintenance Cost

Help maintenance managers, finance controllers, and reliability engineers query and analyze maintenance costs across labor, materials, and contractors. Composes with `maximo-overview`, `maximo-work-orders` (WO cost columns), `maximo-reliability` (cost-per-failure), `maximo-inventory` (material cost context).

## When to use

- "Maintenance cost per asset last year"
- "Top assets by maintenance cost"
- "Cost variance ‚Äî estimate vs actual"
- "Budget vs actual spend by site"
- "PM cost vs CM cost ratio"
- "Contractor spend by vendor"
- "Labor cost vs material cost split"
- "Cost per operating hour for critical assets"
- "Cost trend over time"
- "Cost of unplanned downtime"
- "Bad-actor assets by cost"

For operational backlog / WO count analytics (not cost-focused), use `maximo-work-orders`. For inventory carrying cost / item-level costing methodology, use `maximo-inventory`.

## Pre-flight (per session)

1. **Silver catalog/schema**: confirm via workspace glossary.
2. **Currency**: "Are your sites multi-currency? Does WORKORDER.WOCURRENCY vary?" ‚Äî summing line costs across currencies = wrong number.
3. **Cost rollup convention**: "Do you roll up child-task costs to the parent WO header, or is each WO costed independently?" (Maximo doesn't auto-roll up; customer convention varies.)
4. **Cost categorization**: "How do you distinguish PM from CM in your data?" ‚Äî usually `PMNUM IS NOT NULL` OR `WORKTYPE` codes, sometimes both. Workspace glossary should have the answer.

## Workflow priority

1. **Trusted UDFs** in [metric_udfs.sql](metric_udfs.sql) ‚Äî `asset_maintenance_cost`, `wo_cost_variance_pct`, `pm_vs_cm_cost_ratio`, `cost_per_operating_hour`, `contractor_spend`

3. **Parameterized examples** in [examples.sql](examples.sql)
4. **Raw tables** ‚Äî last resort

## What's intentionally out of scope

- **GL account integration** (`GLACCOUNT`, charge-account joins to customer chart-of-accounts). Very customer-specific. Build a workspace-level skill if a customer needs it.
- **Accounting period transactions** ‚Äî Maximo's accounting integration is via interface tables; out of charter for analytics-side skills.
- **PO / invoice cost** ‚Äî that's procurement (planned v5). This skill stops at WO-side actuals.

## What NOT to do

- **Don't sum `LINECOST` across currencies** ‚Äî `WORKORDER.WOCURRENCY` may vary by site.
- **Don't assume parent WOs roll up child-task costs automatically** ‚Äî they don't. See gotcha 2.
- **Don't filter PM-generated WOs by `WORKTYPE = 'PM'` alone** ‚Äî that's the business categorization, not the source. Use `PMNUM IS NOT NULL` for "generated by a PM"; use `WORKTYPE` for the business intent. Cross-check.
- **Don't include WOs without an asset** in asset-attributed cost reports ‚Äî they materially affect cost-per-asset numbers.
- **Don't conflate `WORKORDER.ACTLABCOST` with `SUM(LABTRANS.LINECOST)`** ‚Äî they should match but often don't. Reconcile and prefer LABTRANS for granular analysis.
