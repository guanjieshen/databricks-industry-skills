## Asset / location hierarchy

- **Region** тЖТ `LOCATIONS` level 1 (top-level parents like `REG-NORTH`, `REG-SOUTH`)
- **Station** тЖТ `LOCATIONS` level 2 (compressor stations, pump stations)
- **Unit** тЖТ `LOCATIONS` level 3 (named units within a station)
- **Equipment** тЖТ leaf-level `ASSET` records

## Criticality conventions

- **Critical** тЖТ `ASSET.CRITICALITY = 10`
- **High** тЖТ `ASSET.CRITICALITY BETWEEN 7 AND 9`
- **Medium** тЖТ `ASSET.CRITICALITY BETWEEN 4 AND 6`
- **Low** тЖТ `ASSET.CRITICALITY <= 3`

## Status conventions

Northstar's definition of "open" work orders:

```sql
WORKORDER.STATUS IN ('WAPPR', 'APPR', 'INPRG', 'WSCH', 'WMATL', 'WPCOND', 'NEEDREV')
```

(`NEEDREV` is a custom status added in 2023 for "needs supervisor review.")

## Tribal knowledge

- "In service" colloquially means `WORKORDER.STATUS = 'INPRG'`, not the formal `ASSET.STATUS = 'OPERATING'`. Disambiguate.
- Region 5 was renamed to "North" in 2024 but the `LOCATIONS` records still say `REG5`. Both terms map to the same site list.
- "Mainline integrity inspections" are tracked as work orders with `WORKTYPE = 'API570'` AND `WO_REG_FLAG = 'Y'`. Other types of inspection work do not have `WO_REG_FLAG = 'Y'` set.
- HSE module is sparsely populated for the first half of 2022 тАФ known data quality gap from a migration. Filter HSE analytics to >= 2022-07-01.
